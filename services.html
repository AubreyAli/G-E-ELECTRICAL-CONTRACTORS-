Our Services

G&E Electrical Contractors provides a comprehensive range of electrical services for residential, commercial, and industrial clients. We are committed to delivering high-quality workmanship, exceptional customer service, and reliable solutions.

Installation

  • Residential Wiring & Electrical Installations: New construction wiring, rewiring existing homes, outlet and switch installations, and more.
  • Commercial Electrical Installations: Office buildings, retail stores, restaurants, and other commercial properties. We handle all aspects of commercial electrical installations, from power distribution to lighting and data systems.
  • Industrial Electrical Installations: Factories, warehouses, and other industrial facilities. We specialize in heavy-duty electrical installations, including motor controls, power distribution, and high-voltage systems.
  • Electrical Fixture Installation: Installation of a wide range of lighting fixtures, including chandeliers, pendant lights, recessed lighting, and more.
  • Appliance Installation: Professional installation of major appliances such as stoves, ovens, dishwashers, and more.

Repair & Maintenance

  • Electrical Troubleshooting & Repairs: Diagnose and repair a wide range of electrical problems, including faulty wiring, short circuits, and power outages.
  • Electrical Panel Upgrades & Replacements: Upgrade your electrical panel to meet current safety standards and accommodate increased energy demands.
  • Wiring Repairs & Replacements: Repair or replace damaged wiring to ensure the safety and efficiency of your electrical system.
  • Electrical System Maintenance: Regular maintenance to prevent future problems and ensure the optimal performance of your electrical systems.

Other Services

  • Home Automation Installation: Install and integrate smart home devices such as smart thermostats, lighting controls, and security systems.
  • Security System Installation & Maintenance: Install and maintain security systems, including alarm systems, surveillance cameras, and access control systems.
  • Solar Panel Installation & Maintenance: Design, install, and maintain solar energy systems for homes and businesses.

Specializations

  • Residential Electrician Services: Providing a wide range of electrical services for homeowners, from minor repairs to major renovations.
  • Commercial Electrician Services: Extensive experience in handling all aspects of commercial electrical work, from new construction to renovations and maintenance.
  • Industrial Electrician Services: Specializing in electrical services for industrial facilities, including heavy-duty installations, maintenance, and troubleshooting.
  • Maintenance Electrician Services: Ongoing maintenance services to ensure the safety and efficiency of your electrical systems.
